   *Question:* Is ~29 min clean / ~35–40 lived (push lap) with a ~25 min farm lap the right
   size for a dungeon?
   *Options:* (a) As specced. (b) Shorter (~22 clean — drop one mid row). (c) Longer (~38
   clean — +1 row, +1 fight).
   *Recommendation:* **(a).** It sits mid-band of the locked 30–45 lived window, it's one clean
   sitting (lunch = a whole run, evening = two laps), and the elite-skip farm lap gives the M+
   grind its fast gear. The dial is a row count — cheap to turn after playtest.

   *Question:* DESCENT's fiction names (PROMPT MARKET · THE JAILBREAK · BACKUPS · CAPTCHA ·

   doors, the world outside is earnest," and dungeons hang off **zone** doors. One vocabulary

   identical everywhere; ~8 display strings get a world-skin column (§5 table). Realm doors
   keep their locked DESCENT names.
   *Recommendation:* **(b).** It preserves the earnest-world lock for the cost of eight
   strings, and players still learn one grammar — same icons, same contracts, same slots; only
   the flavor word changes at the door. (Skeptic check: if the skin table ever grows past ~a
   dozen strings, fold back to (a) — the lock isn't worth a localization layer.)

3. **THE KEYSTONE SITE** *(reconciles a locked line)*
   *Question:* PROGRESSION §UNLOCK-2 wrote "ONE keystone, after the 1st boss" — but this
   dungeon's only boss is the final Seal, where a keystone would arrive too late to matter.
   Where does it pay?
   *Options:* (a) **At the ELITE** — keystone 1-of-2 printed on the elite's door, exactly the
   raid's rule; skip every elite, forfeit your keystone (the same do-I-dare squeeze at dungeon
   scale). (b) Guaranteed at the midpoint node, no fight required (closer to UNLOCK-2's letter).

   choice. Flagged **AMEND** to UNLOCK-2's wording either way.

4. **THE JAILBREAK IN DUNGEONS**
   *Question:* Does the curse den exist at this scale (1 per run), or is it raid-only?
   *Options:* (a) Yes — 1 per run, same two-deals-both-halves-printed contract, bites bounded
   to fights so they resolve inside the run. (b) Raid-only — dungeons stay clean.
   *Recommendation:* **(a).** The dungeon is the PUSH surface — greed-for-power under a
   1-life stake is exactly its identity, and at Depth the extra edge is the point. Rule kept:
   no run-long timing curse; at dungeon scale even DECK TAX bites must print an exit *inside*
   the run (the Market or the Cooling fork — both in the slate).

5. **THE SUBSET TABLE v1** *(Bill's own variety idea, made concrete)*
   *Question:* Approve the per-dungeon system subsets in §9 — Dungeon 1 = Creed-only (creed +

   *Options:* approve / reorder the flavors / start Dungeon 1 on a different subset.
   *Recommendation:* **Approve.** Creed-only first is the teaching subset (fewest mid-run
   ceremonies on the mobile-first surface), Module-not-Creed second makes the two dungeons feel
   like different games (your variety goal), full-stack third is the "heroic" flavor when both
   are proven.

6. **DUNGEON 1's IDENTITY** *(the "our Deadmines" pick — flavor only, authoring stays W3)*
   *Question:* Name and register for the first dungeon, behind Zone 1's east door.
   *Options:* (a) **THE UNDERGRANARY** — the vault-warrens beneath the Gildfields tithe-barns;

   BLACK TITHE (same theme, darker register; boss THE TITHE-WARDEN). (c) Name it yourself —

   *Recommendation:* **(a).** It grows straight out of the zone fiction (the Granary/Warren

7. **THE DEPTH RATCHET AT THE DOOR**
   *Question:* After a clear at Depth N, does the door auto-offer N+1 (push cadence enforced),

   *Options:* (a) Auto-offer N+1, one-tap accept. (b) **Free-pick any unlocked Depth, N+1

   *Recommendation:* **(a).** Same call as the raid's Verdict #12, same reason: the map, door

## 0 · THE DUNGEON IN ONE PARAGRAPH

A dungeon is **one floor of the raid's grammar behind a zone door, with the stakes inverted**:
where the raid is long, checkpointed, and forgiving (BACKUPS), the dungeon is **~29 minutes,
one life, from scratch** — the roguelike core at its purest, and therefore the surface you
**push**. The door carries both dials — **VERSION** (discrete authored mechanic-adds, fake
patch notes) and **DEPTH** (the continuous procedural scalar, primary home here) — plus your
best-Depth standing, so every entry is a stated bet. Inside: the same seeded 3-lane map, the
same printed node contracts, the same three meters, one Market node, one Jailbreak, one elite
holding your keystone, and a **named boss** ending the run at 6–7 minutes. Each dungeon runs a
**subset** of the in-run systems (Dungeon 1 is Creed-only), so different dungeons are different
flavors of the same game, not smaller copies of the raid. Clear it and the door offers you one
deeper. Nothing persists but what always persists: XP, unlocks, oaths, standing — and your
best Depth.

## 1 · TIME BUDGET

**Target: ~29 min clean at good tier (push lap, elite taken) · ~25 min farm lap (elite
skipped) · ~35–40 lived** (shopping, thinking; there is no re-pull — a wipe IS the restart).
Mid-band of WORLD-PLAN's locked 30–45 lived window. Expert ~25 push / ~21 farm.

| Segment | Minutes | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Combat (5 fights) | 15.5 | entry 1.25 + 2 normals ×1.75 + elite 4 + Seal 6.5 (§3) |
| Post-fight ceremony | 4.5 | ~0.9/fight — recap + your draft + 3 AI drafts, dungeon-light |
| Valley nodes (~2 walked) | 3.5 | Market / Cooling / Event / skill node |
| Door ceremony | 1.5 | dials + creed pick + QUEUE-lite board (§8) |
| Arming + oath | 1.5 | one light oath + the charge cash-in at the Seal |
| Routing | 2.0 | 7 rows × ~0.3 — map reads fast or the lap cadence dies |
| **Clean total** | **~28.5** | combat share ~54% — denser than raid floors, right for the push surface |

**The fatigue laws, dungeon-scale:**
- Combat share may run to ~55% (vs the raid's 52% ceiling) — a single sitting doesn't
  accumulate raid fatigue, and the farm lap self-selects for players who want density.
- The pre-Seal valley band and no-stacked-spikes generation rules apply verbatim.
- **Depth must not balloon length** (design constraint the Depth thread inherits): affix depth
  compresses windows and adds beats — it never adds fights or minutes. The push cadence lives
  and dies on the ~30-min lap. Versions may add ≤1 min each to the Seal (authored mechanics),
  nothing else.

**Session model:** SUSPEND anywhere (locked, verbatim). The natural sitting IS the run.
Mobile-first surface — this budget is the phone budget (ABILITY LAW touch ceiling binds here
hardest; §13).

|---|---|---|
| Rows | 8 | **7** (entry + 5 mid rows + Seal) |

| Walked route | 8 | **7** |
| Lanes / crossings / spine | 3 / 45% / same-lane | identical |

| Seeding | run seed | run seed (never wall clock) — replay-stable, shareable co-op maps |

**Mid-row quota bag (15 mids):** COMBAT 6 · ELITE 1 · EVENT 2 · COOLING 1 · CACHE 1 · MARKET 1
· JAILBREAK 1 · SKILL (Trial/Proving rotate) 1 · WILD 1. Generation guarantees: MARKET and the
ELITE reachable from every row-1 position · ≥1 COOLING mid-run · the pre-Seal valley band ·
elite never adjacent to the Seal. **One legible risk fork** (the authored diverging row) at
rows 3–4 — at this length it IS the run's routing identity.

**THE DOOR CONTRACT (NEW — the piece no other doc owns).** The zone-side door is a real
screen, and it prints, before commit:
1. **The VERSION dial** — v1/v2/v3… with fake patch notes, each line a real authored
   mechanic-add on the named boss ("v2: THE TALLYMAN now audits interrupts"). Discrete ladder,
   Trial-Ladder tech.
2. **The DEPTH dial** — the continuous scalar; free-pick any unlocked Depth with **N+1
   highlighted** after a clear (Verdict #7). Next to it, the **affix preview**: the 2–3 lines
   Depth adds at this setting (vocabulary = Forge tiers + ticket mutators; the Depth thread
   owns the math, this screen just prints its output).
3. **Your best** — "DEEPEST: 12 (v2)" — the standing scoreboard, per dungeon.
4. **The stake** — "ONE LIFE. Attempt tokens apply." (§7)
5. **The subset banner** — what this dungeon runs: "CREED-ONLY · keystone at the elite" (§9).
No shard gates, no attunement, no lockout — the door is always open; the dials are the only
gate you set yourself.

## 3 · THE FIGHT BUDGET

**5 fights on the push lap** (4 on the farm lap): entry + ~2 quota normals + the elite (by
choice, pay printed) + the Seal. The deck-cycle law holds with the light stack:

| | Entry | Mid normals | ELITE | SEAL |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Deck at arrival | creed + rig | + ~3–5 boons | + ~5–7 boons | + keystone + ~8 boons |
| Length (good) | ~1.25 min | 1.5–2 (avg 1.75) | **~4 min** | **6–7 min** (§4) |
| Composition | solo/skirmish | duo/patrol (PACK engine) | mutator fight, poison printed | the named boss |

- **TRASH CAP verbatim:** nothing but the elite and the Seal exceeds 3 minutes — dungeon
  normals sit naturally under it; growth is pack composition, never sponges.
- **Enrage:** ~1.5–1.7× TTK, same retightening as the raid.
- **Depth scaling:** windows compress, beats add — fight *count* and the cap never move (§1
  law). Versions land on the Seal only.
- ~5 won fights = ~5 boon drafts + 1 keystone: the build arc is real but compressed — which is
  exactly why the subset table (§9) trims ceremony instead of stacking every system.

## 4 · THE SEAL — the named-boss contract

**One named boss per dungeon** (the named-miniboss rule: **Forge body + authored soul** — the
purge's casting pool is gone, these are authored fresh on Forge chassis).

| | Target (good) | Enrage | Today (Forge capstone ref) | The gap = structure owed |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Dungeon 1 Seal | **6–7 min** | ~8.5 min (~30% headroom) | ~2.5–3 min | ~3.5–4 min — one add wave + one phase turn; the v1 boss is deliberately a *learnable mountain*, not a mythic memory test |

- Sits inside WORLD-PLAN's 5–8 min dungeon-Seal band; the raid ladder (5/7/9/12) stays the
  long-form flex above it.
- **Versions add mechanics to THIS fight** — that's what the door's patch notes describe.
  Never +HP (the one dead lever, re-affirmed).
- Charge cash-in kept: the arming panel runs before the pull, compressed (one screen, no
  floor-boundary market phase around it).
- Boss pays the **locked boss-kill reward stack** (base personal: unlock-if-owed + curio; the
  contested need/greed BONUS roll on top) + **META XP + Depth standing**. No raid-style finale
  ceremony — the door screen shows the lap result and offers N+1 (Verdict #7).

## 6 · ECONOMY — compressed, not redesigned

- **Header verbatim:** three meters + wound/curse pips + the pocket row. The governance rule
  (no new currency without retiring one) binds here automatically — the dungeon adds nothing.
- **Faucets ≈ 40% of a raid's:** skill mint over ~5 fights + salvage on repeat laps + one oath
  purse ≈ **8–12⏣/run** vs the same printed Market prices → you afford **1–2 of 6 slots**.
  Scarcity is the compression; no dungeon-specific price table to maintain. (Add a
  `tokens@market` diagnostic column to the dungeon preset's map-sim run, same as the raid's.)
- **The +1 ATTEMPT Market slot** is the dungeon's marquee purchase (§7) — cap 1/run.
- **Charge:** faucets retuned to the 1-floor scale so the Seal arming lands ~60–80% charged at
  good play — the cash-in stays a decision, not an autopilot (walker generosity flag inherited
  from DESCENT §13).
- **Repeat-lap honesty (the farm surface):** salvage-on-repeats is the anti-inflation valve —
  repeat boon picks convert to ⏣, and the draft-saturation valve (UPSELL conversion) matters
  MORE here than in the raid. Both BUILT-KEEP, both pointed at this surface.

## 7 · STAKES — one life, priced defiance

- **Default: 0 ATTEMPTS in the bank — a wipe ends the run** (locked; from-scratch is the point
  at this length, and the restart is a 30-second walk to the door).
- **Attempt tokens (+1 ATTEMPT)** — the cross-surface consumable, verbatim from the stakes
  model: **earned** at the ticket turn-in fork (instead of the printed reward) or **bought**
  (Market slot, cap 1/run). Spending one at a dungeon wipe = res at the map, boss re-pull —
  Monster-Hunter carts, self-bought.
- **Oaths bank win-or-lose** (locked) — a dead lap is never nothing. One light oath per run,
  taken at the door or the arming panel, so the wipe still writes a Ledger row.
- **Depth standing** records your deepest CLEAR, not your deepest attempt — the scoreboard
  never rewards corpse-checking.
- *(Playtest note, standing from WORLD-PLAN: if 1-life proves too spiky for the kid,
  the sanctioned knob is a starting ATTEMPT at Depth 0 only — never deeper.)*

## 8 · TICKETS — QUEUE-lite, run ledger only

- **The board at the door:** 1–2 tickets offered (the raid's QUEUE, one screen, no floors).
  Every ticket prints its reward at pickup; the turn-in fork (**printed reward OR +1 ATTEMPT**)
  is verbatim — in a 1-life surface that fork finally has teeth.
- **Shape mix (dungeon-weighted):** DEED tickets are the stars ("zero missed parries through
  the elite → a RECAST charge") — performance reads fit a push surface; ROUTE tickets work
  verbatim (lane commitment, forfeit-on-change); ESCORT rides the same port the raid takes
  (one payload, one mutator, fat turn-in). No SEV-1 (cross-floor plumbing has no floors).
- **The one-way bridge, restated:** zone DOOR tickets may READ seed-verified dungeon results
  ("clear THE UNDERGRANARY at Depth 5") and pay world-side — the collection lane's favorite
  food. Run tickets never persist; the two ledgers never mix. (This is the locked Split doing
  its job — dungeons are its main customer.)

## 13 · LANDING NOTES (build order · collisions · risks)

**Sequencing (hard):**
1. **After THE PURGE merges** (shares map-layer deletions), and
2. **after DESCENT's one-bang `raid_map_sim` re-baseline** — the dungeon preset rides the same
   generator inputs and invariants; landing it inside or immediately after that bang avoids a
   second baseline. The preset adds its own invariants to the same suite: elite+market
   reachability · pre-Seal valley band · keystone-at-elite · **a 7-row/17-node/1-Seal shape
   assert**.
3. Dungeon 1 authoring (nodes + THE TALLYMAN) is the **W3 claim** as already phased; this spec
   just de-risks it to content work.

**Collisions:**
- `draft.gd` claim queue (BUILD-LEDGER §0 hotspot): the dungeon adds NO new roll mechanics —
  it consumes RECAST/REGENERATE, the keystone offer, and salvage as built/queued. Zero new
  rows on that pipeline. (Deliberate — the queue is the project's worst hotspot.)
- `world_shell` owns the door screen (boot/world/lobby surface) — the door contract (§2) is a
  world_shell claim, not a raid_hud one.
- The Depth thread: §2's affix-preview lines are RENDERED here, SPECCED there. If that thread
  changes the scalar's vocabulary, only the door strings move.
- The skin table (§5) touches display strings in draft/market/curse surfaces — coordinate with
  any live HUD claims; it's ~8 lookups behind one function, not a sweep.

**Risks / must-flag:**
- **Repeat-lap content fatigue** is this surface's #1 risk (the raid amortizes authored
  content over 3 h; the dungeon over 25 min × many laps). The mitigations are structural
  (quota shuffle, risk fork, WILD, Depth affixes) but the EVENT pool is thin — the
  events-content pass matters more here than anywhere. Flag it in that pass's brief.
- Ceremony time at dungeon scale is unmeasured (same gap as the raid's — one probe serves
  both; DESCENT already asks for it).
- Mobile: the door screen + map taps + the arming panel all live under the touch-target
  ceiling — the W3 mobile layout check is on the critical path, not a polish item.
- Charge faucet retune (§6) shares the walker-generosity fix with DESCENT — one retune, two
  presets.
- If playtest shows elites skipped on push laps (budget assumes taken), the lap drops ~4 min
  and Verdict #3's forfeit rule is doing its job — revisit only if keystone acquisition falls
  below ~70% of laps.
