# Compact screenprint HUD — round 01

`compact_screenprint_hud_v1.png` is a UI-only concept sheet. It deliberately
contains no environment or actors. It applies the bright editorial screenprint
language of Bill's `mainStyle.png`, plus a small dose of the bolder geometry in
`secondaryStyle.png`, to the compact layout verdict:

- dominant Answer Channel;
- five combo sockets attached to its top edge;
- Wind attached to its bottom edge;
- vertical Health and Aggro rails on the sides;
- five compact ability cards directly below;
- compact party, boss, cast, and utility islands above.

## Asset vs live-code seam

Painted assets may own paper housings, corner/end caps, connector wedges,
socket bodies, button-card bodies, key tabs, and static pictograms. Code must
own moving timing targets, grade windows, impact/press marks, all fills and
numbers, combo state, cooldown wipes, readiness/press reactions, verdicts,
feints, charge mode, and flurry mode. The concept is a component target, never
a single baked HUD texture.
