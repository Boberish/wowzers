## The finds

1. **Plate spinning ≠ juggling (Bomber Crew's design language).** Juggling forces equal
   attention on every ball; plate spinning lets you PRIORITIZE — a wobbling plate can wait, a
   falling one can't, and interactions must be quick so attention returns to the whole. The
   Brew's see-saw is a two-plate spin; the unclaimed greed is DELIBERATE wobble — letting one
   side decay toward empty and catching it late, on purpose, for a premium.
2. **The kick is a mind-game with skill on both sides (WoW arena juking).** Fake-casting exists
   because interrupts are scarce and priced — the kicker reads, the caster baits. Our
   interrupt-by-ability pillar (#3) makes the Brew's Spitfire the designated carrier; a theme
   that builds the WEAVE (darts between pours) + deliberate-kick mastery gives the pillar its
   first draft-side surface. (Dependency: the engine flag is pillar work, locked but unbuilt.)
3. **(KB) Fight-arc reading is an untouched clock roster-wide.** Execute thresholds (Killing
   Draught), phase transitions (Last Call) exist as isolated cards; no theme anywhere keys the
   BOSS'S ARC. HP milestones exist in every fight; phases are the raid bonus.

## STEAL CANDIDATES mapped to our grammar (Brew-flavored)

- **Deliberate wobble → THE TIGHTROPE theme:** near-empty catches (a side caught under a
  threshold with a sweet pour) pay premiums — imbalance as CHOSEN greed on the min() core,
  never an easing of it (the Balance lane stays empty by law).
- **The weave → THE SIDEARM theme:** Spitfire riders (darts landed mid-reaction), deliberate
  interrupt mastery, kick-window premiums — comp-texture identity (the caster IS a carrier).
- **The arc → THE PROGNOSIS theme:** absorbs Killing Draught + Last Call; cards keyed to HP
  milestones you PREPARE for (pre-stack before 50%, ripe Rupture into the transition).
- **Plate-spinning interaction speed → a design bar for all three:** every new card's
  interaction must resolve in one pour/tap — nothing that parks attention off the see-saw.
