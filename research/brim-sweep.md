# brim-sweep — fresh search fuel for the Well·BRIM branch slate (2026-07-10)

**Scope.** The grade-the-LANDING healer (`MENDER-PLAN.md` §1): charges Well + pulse refills,
Flash/Mend/Cascade sizing, perfect pour → ✧ GLINT on the ally, spill = counted waste, Brink/
Foresight creeds verdicted, ⭐Reservoir/Triage/Benediction modules re-homed. Deck exists in code
but is branch-thin — this sweep feeds naming + theme pitches. Known class risk (memory): the
raid healer runs IDLE 93–98% — themes that give quiet time a job are worth extra.

## The finds

1. **Trauma Center — grade every routine step.** The surgery games rate EVERY action, not just
   the save — precision on chores is the tension engine, and multi-step OPERATIONS (drain → cut
   → suture) chain graded steps into one held-breath sequence. Validates every-pour-graded;
   suggests an operation-shaped big-CD moment (the BRIMFUL tier's natural home).
2. **FFXIV's green-DPS debate — the warning.** Healers with idle time drift into joyless
   damage-filler identity; the community's own fix direction is fewer, MORE THEMATIC tools and
   letting healing itself carry the job. The legal answer for us: the healer's damage output is
   DELIVERED THROUGH clean healing (the Glint), never through a parallel caster rotation.
3. **Overwatch Ana — no free value.** The beloved skill-healer has zero non-mechanical value:
   every heal is aimed. The steal-shapes: precision-as-identity greed (our blindfold boon is
   already this — a theme can own it), and the biotic-grenade WINDOW (a chosen ally briefly
   receives amplified healing — a set-up-then-pour two-beat play).

## STEAL CANDIDATES mapped to our grammar (Brim-flavored)

- **Grade-the-chore (Trauma Center) → the precision ladder**: blindfold cards, Still-Point-kin
  hairline tiers, operation-chained pours (3 clean pours on 3 different allies = a sequence pay).
- **The green-DPS warning → the GLINTSMITH shape**: all outward damage rides ✧ Glints on allies —
  the idle-healer minutes become Glint-uptime management, never a filler nuke.
- **Ana's grenade → the primed-window play**: spend a beat to PRIME an ally (short amplified-
  landing window), then size the pour into it — self-authored setup greed. (dials: the landing,
  the pour)
- **Deliberate overshoot (Reservoir) → waste-as-fuel pick-tension**: the spec grades zero-spill
  as perfect while the ⭐module BANKS spill — a per-cast fork (Glint or bank) that is the
  overflow build's whole identity.
