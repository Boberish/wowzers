# cask-sweep — fresh search fuel for the Alchemist·CASK branch slate (2026-07-10)

**Scope.** The RECIPE brewer (`ALCHEMIST-PLAN.md` §7): stack graded pours into a cask → seal →

## The finds

1. **Potion Craft — the recipe is a PLOTTED PATH.** Every ingredient bends the cauldron-path a
   known way; brewing = routing around ruin-spots to a destination. The satisfaction is
   authorship: you drew the route. Validates the Cask's VVRRV band-walk as the identity — and
   suggests the *repeatable house recipe* as a mastery object (you know your route by heart).
2. **Overcooked — batch pipelines under fire.** The thrill is overlapping timers (chop while it
   fries while orders queue); the failure mode is over-commitment. One cook is a task; two
   overlapping cooks are a GAME. Validates multi-cask stagger (the parked Double Barrel) as a
   real theme — and its attention cost as the honest price.
3. **(KB) PoE flasks — banked consumables spent on YOUR timing** are a proven build economy:
   charge on performance, drink at the moment. The Cellar module is already this shape; bottles
   FOR ALLIES would be its unclaimed co-op extension.

## STEAL CANDIDATES mapped to our grammar (Cask-flavored)

- **The house recipe (Potion Craft) → a repetition-mastery theme:** re-brewing your exact last
  recipe pays a skill-gated echo (only if the previous batch peak-tapped clean) — commitment
  greed: the fight may want a different recipe than your house pour.
- **The pipeline (Overcooked) → the stagger theme:** two casks in flight (absorbs the parked
  Double Barrel) — stack one while one cooks; payoffs for clean handoffs; attention as the price.
- **Flask-consumables-for-the-party (PoE + Cellar) → the service theme:** bottled peaks thrown
  to allies, applied on THEIR next clean moment (no new ally input) — the co-op shape only a
  4-seat warband can print.
- **Potion Craft's tactile pour → HUD garnish only** (slice-3 territory, not cards): the recipe
  beads already planned; no card should hang on presentation.
