## The finds

1. **Superhot — stillness is the power source.** "Time moves when you move": standing still is
   not passivity, it's the strongest state in the game — but time never fully stops, so
   stillness is SPENT, not parked. The v5 rest-bank (Shades accrue while idle) and First Note/
   Rested (a draw ≥1.5s after the last resolve pays) are this grammar; a REST-economy ladder is
   the fermata fantasy itself (the musical pause IS the move).
2. **Guile — the charge economy.** Charge characters bank power by HOLDING a position (a real
   cost: mobility/options), and the expert tech is charge PARTITIONING — keeping your charge
   while doing other things (SFV Faultless Move: "keep the charge while moving forward").
   Validates coil-cost-as-positioning; steal-shape: upgrades that let you carry a draw through
   other actions are Opus-tier tech, not base (Vanish-Opus already keeps the coil through a
   hit — the one sanctioned carry).
3. **(Both) The bank must be visible and spendable, never safe:** Superhot's time creeps,
   Guile's charge dies when he walks — every banked state needs a leak or a cliff. v5 agrees
   everywhere (Shades spend on next release, Brink zeroes on snap, the coil snaps past the lip).
