# duelist-sweep — fresh search fuel for the Tank·DUELIST challenger slate (2026-07-10)

**Scope.** The dodge tank (`TANK-PLAN.md` §1b): DODGE bread + 60ms PARRY commit, WEAVE through
flurries (all-or-eat-all → free RIPOSTE), eats UNAVOIDABLES, small fast wind pool, low HP that
swings fast, ◆→DUMP. Deck v1 (Headsman/Ironside/Ghost + Dancer) is AT BILL'S BOARD — this sweep
feeds CHALLENGER themes for the same verdict. Fresh angles only (Sekiro-adjacent basics + LoP
are already in `wildcards.md`/`warden-sweep.md`). Point-in-time notes.

## The finds

1. **Sekiro — the deflect streak + win-by-posture.** Deflects in QUICK SUCCESSION do more posture
   damage — the streak itself escalates, and the intended win is a posture-break, not an HP
   grind. The dance framing (turn-taking offense/defense) is the Duelist's exact fantasy. (⚠ a
   boss-side posture bar is engine debt — parked with the old Counterpoint pitch; the legal
   theme-tier steal is the ESCALATING STREAK, kit-local.)
2. **Street Fighter 6 — the Drive economy + BURNOUT.** One gauge feeds parry/rush/impact;
   overspending self-inflicts BURNOUT (chip damage, worse defense, corner stun risk) — the
   crash is authored by your own spending, and managing near-empty is the expert line. Maps
   straight onto the Crucible's ignite→crash cycle as boon material (spend-to-the-brink shapes),
   not a new theme.
3. **Punch-Out!! (Wii) — the bait-and-tell puzzle.** Every attack is telegraphed with a TELL;
   some boxers can ONLY be beaten by baiting; attacking during a TAUNT earns a star (the finisher
   currency). The whole game is reads: pattern → tell → the exactly-right answer. The Duelist's
   feints (hollow bars: READ if ignored, BAITED if answered) are this puzzle — and nothing in
   deck v1 pays the read as a build.
4. **Nine Sols — parries mint charges spent on planted detonations.** Precise parry (0.133s!)
   mints Qi; Qi charges a talisman ATTACHED to the enemy that detonates, converting accumulated
   internal damage to real. Defense-mints-ammo-spent-as-a-payload. (⚠ plant-and-detonate
   triple-collides here: Fermata's Mark + Tempo's Wound theme + the Warden's Payload — killed
   for the Duelist, noted in skipped-on-purpose.)

## STEAL CANDIDATES mapped to our grammar (Duelist-flavored)

- **The bait puzzle → a READ/FEINT theme** (the Matador): insight stacks from correct reads +
  last-instant answers; taunt-punish moments; absorbs `Read the Room`. Pays NOT-pressing — a
  clock no other tank theme uses. (dials: the read, the swing)
- **The escalating deflect streak → chain-shapes** inside existing ladders (Sekiro): streak
  escalation is already half-present in The Rally (every-3rd-land doubles) — a challenger can
  deepen streaks WITHOUT a boss-side bar. (dials: the swing, the bank)
- **Burnout brinkmanship → Crucible/Ironside boon shapes** (SF6): spend-to-the-brink cards with
  a self-authored crash — near-empty wind as the expert line, priced per use. (dials: the wind)
- **The weave/riposte event → a FLURRY-specialist theme** (Stormweave): deck v1 leaves the
  weave→riposte instrument unpaid; flurry appetite + riposte riders = an execution-burst theme
  distinct from the Warden's HOLD answer to the same bars. (dials: the step, the spend)
- **HP as a spendable resource → a BLOOD theme** (Scarlet Trade): the Duelist is the only kit
  that EATS unavoidables by design — Blood Price/Overreach already trade HP for value; a theme
  makes the trade the build. No self-heal ever; the healer duet prices it. (dials: the duet,
  the bank)
