## The finds

1. **Guitar Hero sustains — holds are playable, not idle.** Extended sustains let you play OTHER
   notes while holding one; the whammy adds interaction DURING the hold; and difficulty design
   trades sustain-proportion for tap-density as skill rises. Steal: a held heal (the Vigil) that
   still lets small casts through is a real two-hands skill surface, not a wait.
2. **osu! sliders — press-and-FOLLOW.** The slider is a hold that MOVES: stay with the ball
   through ticks or drop it. The Eddy creed (drifting band) is this grammar on a cast bar —
   following a moving window is its own skill axis, distinct from hitting a fixed one.
3. **Archery draws — hold-too-long is a visible tension curve.** The Pathless: "timing, not
   aiming" (hold duration = quality). Gothic/KC:D: over-hold = sway/stamina wobble; Destiny 2:
   auto-release. Validates the Draw's overrun/gutter design; steal: the over-hold degradation
   should be a READABLE curve (the drawn heal visibly trembles toward the gutter), and stamina-
   priced holds prevent camping the perfect moment.

## STEAL CANDIDATES mapped to our grammar (Draw-flavored)

- **Extended sustains → the Vigil's second hand:** while walking with a held heal, Flash-sized
  casts remain available (module-tier upgrade) — the hold becomes a stance, not a lockout.
- **Slider-follow → the Eddy theme's spine:** the drifting band as press-and-follow; ticks along
  the drift (micro-grades mid-cast) are the osu steal if the deck pass wants depth.
- **The visible tremble → gutter telegraphy:** the held heal's decay toward gutter is drawn ON
  the bar (archery sway) — tension the player reads, never a hidden timer.
- **Density-vs-sustain difficulty → the Rapids/Vigil polarity:** speed builds want tap-density,
  hold builds want sustain windows — two builds, two chart styles, one spec.
