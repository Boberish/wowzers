- **THE TIMER CONTRACT (DESCENT §4):** VORATHEK **5:00** (enrage ~6:30) · MISTRAL **7:00**
  (~9:00) · GEMINI **9:00** (~11:30) · MYTHOS **12:00** (~15:00), at good tier. Growth =
  structure, **never +HP sponge** (add/act HP that gates a new decision is structure; a bigger
  bar on the same loop is not). Do NOT bridge with `--fightlen` (fatigue, not content).
- **COMBAT PILLARS (WORLD-PLAN):** single target law (one telegraph stream — adds/acts/owned
  bodies fine, never two simultaneous answer sources) · dodge ration (~3–8 beats/fight
  non-tank; tanks densest; **ULTRATHINK stays whole** — the documented exception) · interrupt-
  by-ability posture (see KICK POSTURE below) · warband law (tuned for exactly 4 seats) ·
  one spacebar dodge.
- **THE TWO-STREAM LAW (Bill, 2026-07-10):** `enc.melee` = the INVISIBLE auto-attack — chip on
  the aggro-holder only, un-freezable, never telegraphed; it IS the tank's tempo and the
  healer's baseline work. **Everything authored is a telegraph** and lands in a declared lane
  (§4). No new mechanic may blur the seam.
- **TANK CONTENT DESIGNS AGAINST DUELIST/WARDEN** (TANK-PLAN §1b): bar grammar = small/normal/
  tall/flurry/unavoidable/feint; MAIN answers anything + perfect-MAIN hits back; SECONDARY is
  the bread. Bulwark is ignored everywhere in this doc (dies with the Duelist base merge).
  ⚠ The Warden has no dodge — any "must-dodge" raid beat is answered by his SHIELD by design
  (PIERCE is a Depth affix, not a Seal mechanic).
- **KICK POSTURE:** the full contract is **§1½** (Bill, 2026-07-10 — every class but healers
  carries, warn-early/short-window/free-press, BIG miss consequences). Interim (until the
  `interrupts` flag lands class-by-class): verses stay survivable uncontested; the §1½
  consequence numbers arrive WITH the flag, per Seal slice.
- **VALLEY LAW (DESCENT §1):** every fight ≥3 min carries **≥25% of its length as in-fight
  valleys** — walk-ins, stance turns, act curtains, breaks. The §6 scripts each print their
  valley share.
- **TONE + NAMING (MASTER §REALM 1 · THEME-PLAN):** combat is never the joke; dialogue is
  polite/hedging/apologetic; system nouns keep their one global name (dodge/kick/curse/
  Opening); content names wear Realm-1 costume. Vorathek owes its **first-bound-wonder
  re-hang** (THEME §2) — display strings only, in this pass. Retire "rift" from new fiction
  strings. All dialogue/banter = **view-only, never in the checksum**.
- **DETERMINISM:** all new randomness through seeded streams inside `update()` in fixed order;
  breaks/acts are fixed-tick; text is display-only; every new primitive is guarded so
  untouched content stays byte-identical (flag absent ⇒ identical).

## 1 · THE AGGRO DECISION — THE TAUNT BUTTON IS DEAD (Bill, 2026-07-10 — LOCKED)

**The decision.** No taunt button, on any seat, ever. Aggro is **100% passive**: the tank's
FLOW (clean-answer streak, TANK-PLAN §1c) is the boss's attention; slip low and attacks peel;
**the only way back is to play clean** — flow re-climbs, the boss re-locks. No hard override,
no clutch-grab, no tank-swap choreography. *Why (Bill):* taunt makes losing aggro free ("just
taunt back") — WoW-style swaps import risk-free tanking; the dps seats already have early-dodge
+ full rotations to play. The fun budget goes to boss mechanics instead (§5/§6 — the mark
relay, stance reads, act rules are the "other fun mechanics" replacing taunt tech).

**What stays (unchanged from §1c):** flow moves on SKILL never on hits · ≥30% locked on tank,
<30% rising per-attack peel chance, 0% random · a peel is telegraphed on the victim's OWN dodge
bar + warning + fixed grace-delay · non-tanks carry low passive threat (damage/heals) — that
secondary table picks WHO catches a peel · no tank ⇒ aggro sits low ⇒ everyone shares
(emergent hard-mode) · numbers = playtest (flow gain/loss/decay are SealTune-exposed, §2).

**What changes:**
- **The grace-delay is now the VICTIM'S window only** (react + dodge). It stops being a
  "taunt-it-back valve" — a peel always costs the raid one real answer. This is the risk Bill
  wants back in the loop.
- **The recovery valve is skill-shaped (V#9):** perfect MAIN (parry/shield-slam) grants a flow
  spike — the counter is the "look at me". Plus the aggro boon lane (LODESTONE · HARD STARE,
  💡 in CARD-CATALOG) for players who want insurance. No button anywhere.
- **THREAT_DROP re-based on flow:** the curse effect becomes **FLOW DUMP** — "Context
  Compaction" sets the tank's flow to 0 (the boss literally forgets them); the tank rebuilds
  from zero while the raid dodges the peels. Better drama than taunt-back, same fiction.
  Vorathek's Baleful Curse (the old THREAT_DROP teacher) is parked (V#2) — the concept lives
  at Mythos where the raid can already read peels.
- **Hot-potato-by-taunt dies** (WORLD-PLAN:302's "tank-swaps, hot-potato curses" expression) —
  replaced by the boss-authored MARK RELAY (V#8), which is hot-potato as a *timed answer*, no
  taunt required. A JAILBREAK curse variant can reuse the same primitive later.
- **Code fallout (build-time, rides Wave 1):** `CombatCore.taunt()` + `taunt_seat_i`/
  `taunt_until_tick` + `BulwarkKit._challenge` + `challenge_cd` + every T-CHALLENGE UI string
  (`tank_band` · `class_codex` · `raid_hud` banner · `world_shell` blurb · rig hook) + the
  `raid_sim` taunt A/B probe die or re-key to flow. Most of it is Bulwark, which dies in the
  same merge. `BossState.threat` (table) + `_threat_target()` survive re-sourced; the peel
  roll + flow driver are the Wave-1 build (BUILD-LEDGER §B), not this plan's.

**Doc amendments carried in this commit:** TANK-PLAN §1c/§1d taunt lines + §8.0 Warden button
budget (TAUNT removed → 4 of 7) · WORLD-PLAN:302 supersession note · BUILD-LEDGER §0/§B/§F
rows · CARD-CATALOG 2 aggro-boon 💡 rows. MASTER-PLAN §CLASSES' 2026-07-09 "TAUNT = hard
override" log entry stands as history; this doc + the 2026-07-10 log entry govern.

## 1½ · THE KICK CONTRACT (Bill, 2026-07-10 — the interrupt spec the fights author against)

**The carrier amendment (supersedes pillar-3's "2/1/0 comp texture"):** **every class except
healers carries a kick**, bolted onto its DUMP (Twinfang: Evis proposal 🟡 TEMPO §17.10 ·
Alchemist: the Spitfire carrier · Duelist/Warden: ⚡ DUMP). Healers never kick. The standard
warband fields **3 kickers**; comp texture moves from *who carries* to **whose dump is armed
when the window lights** — the interrupt tax (hold your payoff or lose the kick) and the
chain rotation are the whole skill. WORLD-PLAN §PILLARS #3 + CLAUDE.md amended this commit.

**The anatomy — warn EARLY, window SMALL, press FREE, miss EXPENSIVE:**
- **The warn is the whole cast.** The castbar + banner appear at cast start (verses cast
  ~2–6 s) — plenty of read time to arm the right dump. (The V#10 ribbon, if built, extends
  the warning earlier; not required.)
- **The window is a small lit slice at the END of the cast** — `kick_window`, an ABSOLUTE
  time knob on SealTune (first cut **~0.6 s** ≈ 18 ticks; per-Seal mult: Mistral ×1.3 the
  kindergarten · Gemini ×1.0 · Mythos ×0.85). This tightens WORLD-PLAN's "last ~35% of cast"
  starting idiom into a fixed slice — because the press is free, the window carries ALL the
  skill. Tunable, like everything (§2).
- **The press is free, the timing isn't.** Pressing your dump EARLY just fires it as normal
  damage (no kick, no penalty) — the cost is your dump may not be re-armed when the slice
  lights. First in-window press consumes the verse (simultaneous same-tick presses resolve in
  seat order, deterministic; the loser's dump fires as damage). Silence-kills-chain semantics
  unchanged.
- **Missing is the costliest single mistake in the raid** (Bill: "make the consequences
  big") — a landed verse must out-hurt any missed dodge/parry. The ladder:
  | Seal | Missed-kick consequence (first cut — all knobs, re-tuned when the flag lands) |
  |---|---|
  | VORATHEK | **Devouring Chant lands = the boss HEALS a chunk** ("restoring from checkpoint") — the widest window, the gentlest miss: recoverable, but you feel the progress slip. The teaching version of Mythos's Hotfix (boss-heal bookends the raid). |
  | MISTRAL | License verse lands = the **biggest single raid blast in the fight** (heal-scramble tier, recoverable — the loud lesson) |
  | GEMINI | Overview verses = raid blasts; the **Model Merge lands = permanent EMPOWER stack** — every miss makes the rest of the fight worse, and stacks compound |
  | MYTHOS | CoT Conclusion = the biggest EMPOWER in the raid; **OPUS Hotfix lands = the boss HEALS** (direct progress loss — the classic worst outcome); an Act-III verse at near-lethal |
- **AI seats kick too** (policies hold dumps for incoming verses; miss rates are the skill
  tier — sloppy AI misses kicks, that's the comp texture in solo).
- **Sim gates (rides S0):** the pillar's accidental-vs-deliberate kick-rate diag becomes a
  per-seat table (landed / missed / uncovered verses per tier; targets: accidental <10%,
  deliberate >85% at good — WORLD-PLAN numbers stand). With early-press-fires-as-damage,
  "accidental" kicks should measure ≈0 — the table proves it.
- **Count stays modest (Bill: "some, not a ton"):** Vorathek **one un-chained Chant** (~2
  casts/fight, widest window — the kindergarten, V#11b) · Mistral one 2-verse chain (~2–3
  casts) · Gemini one 3-verse chain incl. the Merge (~2–3 casts) · Mythos CoT 3-verse (~2
  casts) + Hotfix per OPUS wave. Verses stay INSIDE the §3 beat budgets. The ramp reads
  **single kick → first chain → chain+empower → the exam.**

## 2 · THE TUNING SPINE — playtest-turnable without a playtest

**The problem (Bill):** fight length/speed can't be validated until the raid is playable, so
nothing may bake. **The rule:** every pacing number in §6 lands as data on a per-Seal tune
block, live-turnable via `tune.sh`, asserted by sim gates — the plan ships *shapes*, the
numbers ship *loose*.

**E4 — `SealTune` (per-encounter tuning block, `EncounterRes.tune`):**
| Knob | What it scales | First use |
|---|---|---|
| `hp_mult` · `dmg_mult` | the two coarse dials (today's tune.sh pair, per-Seal) | all |
| `cd_mult` | EVERY ability cd in the encounter (density master dial) | all |
| `melee` override | the tank-tempo triple {every,min,max} | ramp §3 |
| `window_mult` | grade-window width on this Seal's beats (teaching Seal wider) | Vorathek 1.15 |
| `phase_fracs[]` · `act_gates[]` | HP breakpoints for phases and acts | all |
| `stance_period` | expert/voice rotation cadence (V#4) | Mistral · Gemini |
| `break_len[]` | dialogue-break durations in ticks | Gemini · Mythos |
| `beats_scale` | barrage beat-counts (3→2 etc, the last-resort lever) | §3 |
| `peel {grace_ticks, curve, lethality}` · `flow {gain, loss, decay, spike}` | the §1 economy, per-Seal overridable | all |
| `kick_window` (+ per-Seal mult) | the lit kick slice at cast end, ABSOLUTE seconds (§1½; first cut ~0.6 s) | Mistral ×1.3 · Gemini ×1.0 · Mythos ×0.85 |
| `verse_miss_mult` | the missed-kick consequence sizes (blast / EMPOWER / heal amounts, §1½ ladder) | re-tuned when the flag lands |
| `enrage_at` · `enrage_visible` | the wall + whether the countdown banner shows | all |
| `re_stagger` | post-break/act re-arm stagger (kills the banked burst-train, §7 gotcha) | all |

`tune.sh <seal> 30 all --bosslen= --bossdmg= --bosscd= --stance= --break= --peel= ...` grows
the matching flags (~15 s iteration); numbers bake into `raid_content.gd` only when a build
feels right, then the full gate runs.

**Sim gates (Slice 0 — absorbs SEAL-PILLAR Phase A, byte-identical to build):**
1. **Beat-budget table** per seat/Seal/tier (presented · perfect/good/graze/miss · feints) —
   aggregation of `seat.diag`, sim-side only, zero engine files.
2. **TTK line per Seal**: measured good-tier TTK vs the contract target, flagged when outside
   ±20% (a report line, not a hard fail, until Bill locks numbers).
3. **Act/valley timeline**: when each phase/act/break/add actually fired, valley % of fight.
4. Cast counts per beat-source (scheduler-watching, sim-side).
Gate A: determinism PASS ×4 + checksums byte-identical pre-content-change. Every content
slice thereafter is a **deliberate re-baseline** (§8) with fresh 300-seed bands.

## 4 · THE VISUAL GRAMMAR LAW (Bill: "coherent to the game/visuals")

Every mechanic in §6 declares its READ. The lanes that exist: **banner** (ability name + tag) ·
**tank bars** (height grammar §1b) · **victim dodge bar** (peels/rand-targets/marks — always
with warning + grace) · **castbar** (verses; kick flag when kicks exist) · **doom banner**
(SURVIVE IT) · **party frames** (the aggro box: gold = current target). New widgets this pass
adds (all small, all view-only): **stance sigil** (persistent chip by the boss plate — the
live expert/voice, its palette tints the telegraph banners) · **countdown pips** (3·2·1 on a
banner — the interactive charge counter) · **break card** (letterboxed dialogue frame — reads
as theater, never as a telegraph) · **mark fuse** (a ticking pip chain on the marked seat's
frame + handoff beat on their bar, V#8). Rig-side: stance/act flips are PoseRig pose+palette
swaps (cheap on robots — servo posture, monitor glow); adds enter on the PACK walk-in idiom so
"new body approaching" always looks the same. **A mechanic that can't name its lane doesn't
ship.** Every new banner/sigil/break gets a WSLg visual-probe shot before merge (headless
can't render `_draw`).

## 6 · THE FOUR FIGHTS (scripts to the contract — all numbers SealTune-loose)

### SEAL I — VORATHEK (F1 · Ring 3) — **THE AXE** · 5:00 · enrage ~6:30 (visible? no — teach the clock at Mistral)
**Identity:** the first-bound wonder (fiction re-hang owed, display-only): a BEAST, the raid's
only silent boss — slow, huge, honest. Bill's "2H axe guy": fewer, taller, harder-hitting
telegraphs with generous windows. Teaches the grammar: bar heights, ONE raid dodge motion,
the heal check, the Opening, **the kick** (V#11b), the enrage clock. **The learnable loop (S2):** its telegraph
string repeats verbatim — with one slot that only sometimes fires (the first feint-shaped
lesson without a real feint: an empty windup, "READ" if held).
**Vocabulary (V#2 cuts + V#11b Chant):** melee (gentle 1.15) · Crush/Talon talls (tank) · Void
Volley (3-beat aoe string, slow, high-damage, wide windows — THE dodge lesson) · Rift
Cataclysm (nova heal-check, ramps by phase) · **Devouring Chant (the kick teacher, V#11b):**
one un-chained interruptible self-heal on a slow cast (~4 s) with the raid's **widest kick
window** — miss it and the boss restores a chunk ("restoring from checkpoint"). Teaches the
whole kick contract on floor 1; the gentle rehearsal for Mythos's Hotfix. A wordless
growl-cast (still no banter — see Visuals). · **Overload Slam (NEW, S3):** long windup →
one near-lethal aoe beat (mini-doom, 1 beat not 3) → boss STUNNED ~4 s = a printed Opening
the whole warband dumps into. Brace → survive → punish, the game's core loop in one move.
**Structure to 5:00:** walk-in pack (2 riftspawn, PACK idiom — the pull fantasy) ~0:40 →
ACT A loop taught, **first Chant ~50% into it** (100→60%) ~1:30 → **add wave at 60%**
(Broodmaw, one body — teaches "boss withdraws, kill the add") ~0:45 → ACT B (60→30%) loop +
Overload Slam cycle + 2nd Chant ~1:15 → ACT C (30→0) speed 1.2, Volley gains its 4th beat
(S14) ~0:50. Valley share ~27% (walk-in · stun-Openings · add walk-in). **Phases:**
1.0/0.6/0.3 (mult 1.0/1.15/1.3 · speed 1.0/1.1/1.2).
**Seats:** tank = slow tall parry/shield rhythm (the honest teacher) · dps = Volley + Opening
discipline **+ the armed-dump kick on the Chant** (the kick tax, taught soft) · healer =
Cataclysm + melee chip (the §1c scoreboard, gentle) — never kicks.
