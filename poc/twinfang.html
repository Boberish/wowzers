The Twinfang

You're the damage. Kill the boss before it grinds you out — and your damage is a rhythm, not a mash.

1 Strike — tap it as the bar hits the green zone for a Perfect: harder hit, +2 combo, +Flow, faster swing
2 Eviscerate — spend combo points for burst (bigger with more points)
3 Kick — interrupt the boss's heal cast (or it heals and the fight drags)
Space Dodge — evade a telegraphed swing. Getting hit wipes your Flow.
Flow multiplies all your damage — chaining Perfects is the whole skill ceiling.

Choose your Aspect

Two ways to be the blade. This sets your signature mechanic and weights what you're offered between fights.

Tempo
Flow escalation · rhythm
The rhythm virtuoso. As your Flow climbs, your kit transforms in tiers: Perfect Strikes start double-hitting, then refund energy and pour out extra combo — and at max Flow you unlock Coup de Grâce, a huge finisher that refeeds your combo so you can chain straight into another burst. Hold the solo, and the whole kit sings.
Venomancer
Status craft · detonate
Poisons come in three types: normal strikes apply Crippling, perfects apply Virulent, and Envenom lays Festering (which grows the longer it sits). Keep all three alive at once and Toxic Synergy ramps up. Then Rupture detonates the whole cocktail for a burst scaled by everything you've stacked. Setup and payoff, not execution — and it wants you to mix normal and perfect hits.
