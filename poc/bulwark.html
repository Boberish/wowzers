The Bulwark

The tank, no character to steer — just the fight. Damage you take becomes Rage, which fuels your Cleave, Rampage and Fortify. But how you survive is a choice: pick an Aspect.

Choose your Aspect

Two ways to hold the line. This decides your signature mechanic and biases what you're offered between fights.

The Warden
Parry → Riposte · precision
Space becomes Parry — a tighter window than a dodge, but landing one reflects damage, banks a Counter, and opens a Riposte: your next Cleave hits like a truck. Chain parries, then spend Counter on Vindicate to make the boss reel. Reactive, offensive, all about reading the swing.
The Juggernaut
Momentum · greed
Every hit you deal or eat builds Momentum — more damage and more mitigation, the higher you ride. But dodging dumps it, so each big swing is a bet: bail and lose your snowball, or eat it and trust your stacks. Cash out with Avalanche before something you can't afford to eat.
