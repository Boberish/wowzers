The Voidcaller

A ranged caster — no character to steer, just the duel of casts. The boss casts constantly: heals, buffs, nukes. Your job is to read the purple bar and interrupt the one that matters. But your interrupt is on a cooldown, so when two casts overlap you can only stop one. Pick your Aspect.

Choose your Aspect

Two philosophies of the interrupt. This sets your signature mechanic and biases what you're offered between fights.

The Disruptor
Backlash · punish the cast
Every interrupt hits back and banks a Backlash — and a clean interrupt (stopping the cast at the last moment) banks more. You want the boss to cast so you can punish it. Dump your stacks with Overload for a nuke that scales with how many casts you've broken.
The Silencer
Silence · suppress casting
Your interrupts don't just stop one cast — they Silence the boss and leave it Exposed (taking more damage) while it can't cast at all. Chain them to hold the lockout, and pour your hardest casts into the window. Quietus is a hard silence that flings the door wide open.
