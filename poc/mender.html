The Mender — Aspects

You are the healer. You don't steer a character — you keep the party alive while they burn the boss down. Ally damage tracks their health, so the fight ends before your mana does. Two Aspects change how you keep them alive — one plays a beat ahead, one plays a beat behind, on purpose.

Choose your Aspect

Both keep the raid alive. They could not disagree more about how.

The Tidecaller
Reservoir · anticipate
Overhealing isn't wasted — it banks into a Reservoir. Top people off early, cast a beat ahead, and when you see the telegraph, Surge the whole bank into shields right before it lands. The calm, foresighted healer: nothing you cast is ever spilled.
The Brinkwarden
Nerve · gamble
Your heals get huge and nearly free the lower the target — so you let allies ride dangerously low and catch them at the last instant. Bloodied allies even hit harder, and holding them in the red builds Nerve for a raid-saving Last Stand. Play a beat behind, on purpose. Misjudge by half a second and someone dies.
